x - Light Punch

Run(ADV)/Front Step(EX) - F, F

Maximum Mode(ADV) - x+a+y(Requires 1 Power Bar)
Power Charge(EX) - x+a+y
Safe Fall Recovery - x+a when nearing ground while falling
