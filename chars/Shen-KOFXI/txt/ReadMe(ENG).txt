Shen Woo(KOFXI Style) by Ikaruga
for WinMugen / Mugen 1.0

*Original : Jin's Shen + fix patch by me

=====<Movelist>=====

-- Button Assign
x - Light Punch
y - Hard Punch
z - Knockdown Attack
a - Light Kick
b - Hard Kick
c - None
start - Taunt


-- Moves
Knockdown Attack - y+b or z
GC Knockdown Attack - y+b or z while guarding(Requires 1 Power Bar)
Roll - x+a
GC Roll - x+a while guarding(Requires 1 Power Bar)
Quick Roll - x+a while attack hitting(Requires 1 Skill Bar)
Safe Fall Recovery - x+a when nearing ground while falling
Super Cancel - Super Moves -> DM(Requires 1 Skill Bar)
Dream Cancel - DM -> Leader DM(Requires 1 Skill Bar)


*Normal Mode

-- Throw
Kyakuten Touchi - F, y or b


-- Command Moves
Fusen Kyaku         - F, a
 ->Fusen Gouwan Ken - F, y


-- Super Moves
Geki Ken  - D, DF, F, x or y
 ->Cancel - a or b

Fukko Geki     - D, DB, B, x
 ->Kouryu Geki - D, DF, F, x

Dan Ken - D, DB, B, y

Zenpou Tenshin - D, DF, F, z
Kouhou Tenshin - D, DB, B, z
 ->Tenren Ken  - x or y

Saikou Hougeki - F, DF, D, DB, B, F, x or y


-- DM
Zetsu! Geki Ken - D, DF, F, D, DF, F, x or y

Kohyou Rengeki - D, DF, F, D, DF, F, a or b


-- Leader DM
Baku Shin - y, x, a, y or D, D, D, z


*2k3 Mode(select to start + any key)

-- Throw
Kyakuten Touchi - F, y or b


-- Command Moves
Fusen Kyaku - F, a

Tenren Shu - F, b


-- Super Moves
Geki Ken  - D, DF, F, x or y
 ->Cancel - a or b

Fukko Geki     - D, DB, B, x or y
 ->Kouryu Geki - D, DF, F, x or y

Saikou Hougeki - F, DF, D, DB, B, F, x or y


-- DM(Requires 1 Power Bar)
Zetsu! Geki Ken - D, DF, F, D, DF, F, x or y

Kohyou Rengeki - D, DF, F, D, DF, F, a or b


-- Leader DM(Requires 2 Power Bars)
Baku Shin - y, x, a, y
