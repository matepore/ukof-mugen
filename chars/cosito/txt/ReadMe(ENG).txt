for WinMugen / Mugen 1.0

=====<Movelist>=====

-- Button Assign
x - Light Punch
y - Hard Punch
z - Knockdown Attack
a - Light Kick
b - Hard Kick
c - Maximum Mode
start - Taunt


-- Moves
Knockdown Attack - y+b or z
GC Knockdown Attack - y+b or z while guarding
Roll - x+a
GC Roll - x+a while guarding(Requires 1 Power Bar)
Quick Roll - x+a while attack hitting(Requires 1 Power Bar)
Safe Fall Recovery - x+a when nearing ground while falling
Maximum Mode - a+y(Requires 1 Power Bar)
Quick Max - a+y while attack hitting(Requires 2 Power Bars)


-- Throw
Minegumo - F, y

Murakumo - F, b


-- Command Moves
Senkou Sengi: Yukikaze - F, x

Sokutou Sengi: Shigure - F, a

Shukyaku Sengi: Shinonome - DF, b


-- Super Moves
Chisou Gata Basshutou Sengi: Sawarabi - D, DF, F, x or y

Taikuu Gata Basshutou Sengi: Yoitsuki - F, D, DF, x or y

Kyoushu Gata Basshutou Sengi: Tenmu - D, DB, B, a or b


-- DM(Normally:Requires 1 Power Bars/MAX Mode:No Cost)
Senkou Gata Basshutou Ougi: Shakuhou - DB, F, DF, D, DB, B, DF, x or y

Kakusan Gata Basshutou Ougi: Zetsuei - DB, F, DF, D, DB, B, DF, a or b


-- SDM(Normally:Requires 3 Power Bars/MAX Mode:Requires 1 Power Bars)
Kaiten Gata Tokkan Ougi: Rasen - D, F, DF, x+y+a+b


-- MAX2(In low life, Normally:Requires 3 Power Bars/MAX Mode:Requires 1 Power Bars)
Saishu Gata Basshutou Higi: Rinkou -  F, DF, D, DB, B, F, DF, D, DB, B, x+y
