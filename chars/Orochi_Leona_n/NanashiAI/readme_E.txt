How to use

Please put the "NanashiAI" folder and "Orochi_Leona_n.def" in Ahuron's production "Orochi_Leona" folder,
 and register "Orochi_Leona/Orochi_Leona_n.def" into "select.def".

Caution!
This AI don't has AI level.

And if you want AI quick start, rewrite Orochi_Leona_n.cmd's text ↓

--------------------------------------
;Trigger15 = 1;AI即起動スイッチ(Switch that start AI immediately)  (Before)

to

Trigger15 = 1;AI即起動スイッチ(Switch that start AI immediately)   (After)
--------------------------------------

Then , AI is usually start quickly.

However , the switch on ,and you can't control Leona.  

When you need control Leona, the switch should not on. OK ?
